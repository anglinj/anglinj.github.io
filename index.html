About Our Story Telling Game

  • What type of game is it?
    Will you find yourself battling to the death with thousands of enemies surrounding you or will you voyage through the ocean with a crew of pirates? Will you soar through the sky like a mythical hero or will you traverse through space? Where will your stories take you?
    This is a storytelling game that gives players the opportunity to create compelling stories together that will be remembered for years to come. Each card will help dictate how the story will unfold, but it's up to the group’s collective imagination to weave together a truly remarkable tale.
  • It is like D&D?
    Not really as D&D is a table top RPG. This games focuses on the story elements more so than the players taking control of just one character and/or aspect of the story. That being said we are Alpha testing a table top RPG version of the game.
  • When will you guys be doing a play through?
    We just completed the Beta testing phase of the game and we will be doing other play throughs at local games shops soon, we will post them in the events soon. Sorry for the slight delay.
